UC San Diego vs Cal Poly SLO

Saturday, November 1, 2014

The Tritons had a strong start to their weekend in this afternoon game against SLO. They had played the team before in the recent Santa Barbara tournament, tying them 1-1. As the game started the girls came out hard, playing a smart possession game as well as fighting for the ball. However, SLO was matching in intensity. There were many close chances for both teams, including a great shot from team captain, Olivia Hadidian, that went barely wide right of the goal. Both teams fought hard and played a clean game, but when the whistle blew at the end, the score remained 0-0.
