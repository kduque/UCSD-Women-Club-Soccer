About Us

TEAM OVERVIEW

The UC San Diego Women’s Club Soccer Team is part of NIRSA Region VI: West Coast Soccer Association – Premier Central Division, which also includes UC Irvine, UCLA, UC Santa Barbara, and the University of Southern California. We are aware that, as students, missing classes is not ideal. Student athletes must, however, learn to successfully balance soccer and their studies. Although we fully understand that you are a student first, we expect players who have committed to the team to make a concerted effort to attend all practices and games. As athletes, our attendance and work ethic are a reflection of our respect for the visiting teams, our teammates, and the administrators who work to make our program possible. Playing for the UC San Diego Women’s Club Soccer Team is a wonderful experience, a way to have fun, relieve stress, and continue playing competitive soccer against challenging opponents after high school and club soccer end. Our players form close bonds and lasting friendships, a family that we hope to make you a part of.
