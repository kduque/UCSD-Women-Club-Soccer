About Us

TEAM OVERVIEW

The UC San Diego Women’s Club Soccer Team is part of NIRSA Region VI: West Coast Soccer Association – Premier Central Division, which also includes UC Irvine, UCLA, UC Santa Barbara, and the University of Southern California.

We are aware that, as students, missing classes is not ideal. Student athletes must, however, learn to successfully balance soccer and their studies. Although we fully understand that you are a student first, we expect players who have committed to the team to make a concerted effort to attend all practices and games. As athletes, our attendance and work ethic are a reflection of our respect for the visiting teams, our teammates, and the administrators who work to make our program possible.

Playing for the UC San Diego Women’s Club Soccer Team is a wonderful experience, a way to have fun, relieve stress, and continue playing competitive soccer against challenging opponents after high school and club soccer end. Our players form close bonds and lasting friendships, a family that we hope to make you a part of.


SEASON & PRACTICES

Fall Quarter is the busiest time of year: league matches begin the week following tryouts and the team travels to Santa Barbara to participate in the UC Santa Barbara Invitational, in addition to other independently scheduled matches. Based on their league standing and NIRSA rankings, some teams are also invited to play in the Regional Tournament, from which they can qualify to participate in Nationals.

During Winter Quarter, we compete against teams in Southern California, in addition to a weekend of round robin friendlies with schools in and around the Bay Area.

Spring Quarter means Spring League and a variety of university sponsored tournaments, including UCLA’s Bruin Classic and the UCSD Invitational, a successful venture held jointly by the Men’s and Women’s club teams since 2009, which plays host to teams from across the nation.


FEES

There is a fee to play for the women’s team that varies annually. In previous years, it has been approximately $550. Please bear in mind that this amount covers the entire year and all manner of expenses, including but not limited to uniforms, apparel, equipment, tournaments, hotels, gas, and referees.

Please do not be discouraged if this seems financially inaccessible for you. We fund raise throughout the year to help subsidize costs and take pains to ensure no one is barred from playing for purely monetary reasons.
